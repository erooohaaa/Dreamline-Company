Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Dreamline Company Bot is a WhatsApp chatbot used by Dreamline Company to communicate with customers and respond to their requests.

Information We Process

When users contact Dreamline Company Bot through WhatsApp, we may process the following information:

  • WhatsApp phone number;
  • WhatsApp profile name;
  • messages sent by the user;
  • conversation history required to provide customer support.

How We Use Information

We use this information only to respond to customer requests, provide information about services, process inquiries, and improve the quality of communication.

Data Sharing

We do not sell user personal data to third parties. User data may be processed through Meta WhatsApp Business Platform and internal systems required for customer communication.

Data Retention

We keep user data only for as long as necessary to provide support, maintain communication history, or comply with legal and business obligations.

Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their data by contacting us at: dreamline_dlc@proton.me.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: dreamline_dlc@proton.me.
